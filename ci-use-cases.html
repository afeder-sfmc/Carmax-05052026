Advanced

Combining CIs for Powerful Strategies

The real power comes from layering multiple Calculated Insights together. Here are three compound strategies that combine two or more CIs.

🔥 Strategy 1: The "Ready to Buy" Waterfall

Layer Propensity Score ≥ 60 + Engagement Velocity accelerating + CLV PurchaseCount = 0 to identify first-time buyers with strong intent who are actively heating up. This is the highest-priority segment for conversion.

Propensity ≥ 60 + Recent > Prior Week + PurchaseCount = 0 → 🎯 Hot First-Time Buyer

🚗 Strategy 2: The "Perfect Match" Email

Combine Vehicle Preference (body type + price range) with Propensity Score to send hyper-personalized emails. A customer who prefers SUVs under $35K with a high propensity score gets SUV inventory cards with a "Schedule Your Test Drive" CTA.

PreferredBodyType = SUV + AvgPrice ≤ $35K + Score ≥ 50 → 📧 Personalized SUV Email

💎 Strategy 3: The "Loyal VIP Re-Engagement"

Find customers with CLV TotalPurchaseValue > $30K (proven buyers) but Velocity decelerating (going quiet). These high-value customers deserve a premium win-back — a personal call, exclusive offer, or early access to new inventory.

TotalPurchaseValue > $30K + RecentWeek = 0 + PriorWeek > 0 → 💎 VIP Win-Back
