Storyline

CarMax marketers launch an expiring-credits re-engagement campaign in MCA. They draft with Campaign Agent, build the target audience with Einstein Segmentation, then expand inside the same stop with Calculated Insights, engagement data, and waterfall campaign prioritization. They finish with dynamic loop content, orchestration, activation controls, and reporting-based optimization.

Harbor Cruise Run of Show (90 min)

Dock: Business Framing
8 min
  • Define outcomes: conversion, relevance, and pressure control.
  • Set expectations for a product-led walkthrough.
Stop 1: Campaign Agent
15 min
  • Draft campaign from business brief.
  • Validate messaging strategy and channel plan.
Stop 2: Einstein Segmentation + CIs + Engagement + Waterfall
22 min
  • Create audience with Einstein natural language.
  • Refine with value, recency, loyalty, and eligibility rules.
  • Add Calculated Insights and engagement overlays.
  • Demonstrate waterfall prioritization for overlapping campaigns.
Stop 3: Dynamic Loop Email
14 min
  • Show repeaters/loops for personalized modules.
  • Preview multiple personas and content outcomes.
Stop 4: MCA Flow Orchestration
14 min
  • Walk entry criteria, branching, waits, and fallbacks.
  • Show suppression logic and frequency governance.
Stop 5: Activation Controls & QA
8 min
  • Audience checks, approvals, and preflight controls.
  • Operational confidence before send.
Stop 6: Reporting & Optimization
6 min
  • Show campaign and journey outcomes.
  • Connect results to next audience iteration.
Wrap: Executive Recap
3 min
  • Restate value: speed + precision + governance.
  • Confirm readiness for scaled adoption.

Use Cases in the Demo

  • Primary: Expiring rewards credits re-engagement with service-ready offer.
  • Feature moment: Dynamic loop email with personalized repeated blocks.
  • Governance moment: Engagement/frequency suppression + waterfall prioritization.
  • Keep Stop 2 framed as one cohesive decisioning block.

Data Cloud Scope (Deliberately Narrow)

Show This

  • Einstein segment criteria and output quality.
  • Calculated Insights used in send qualification/ranking.
  • Engagement signals used for suppressions/exclusions.
  • Waterfall method for campaign prioritization.

Avoid This

  • Long ingestion and identity architecture detours.
  • CDP replacement positioning discussions.
  • Engineering-heavy setup details during business storyline.
